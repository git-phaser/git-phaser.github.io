A Social Proximity App for GitHub

Git-Phaser uses iBeacons to make your public GitHub profile
visible to anyone within a 50ft radius of you.

See the repos of people around you.

Have a history of people you've met.

Add them to your device contacts.

Proximity detects with Bluetooth LE.

Finds users even if their app is closed.

Good for meetups and conferences.

Free and open source.

Does not track by location.

Does not share your data.

Does not drain your battery.
