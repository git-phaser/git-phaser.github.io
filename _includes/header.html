A Social Proximity App for GitHub

Sign in once. From then on anyone using git-phaser near you will be able to see your public GitHub profile, browse your repos and add you to their contacts. You'll also get notifications when users are nearby and you'll have a history of the people you've crossed paths with.

Proximity detects with Bluetooth.

Finds users even if their app is closed.

Good for meetups and conferences.

Free and open source.

Does not track you by location.

Does not share data about you.

Does not drain your battery.
