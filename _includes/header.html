A Social Proximity App for GitHub

Git-Phaser lets anyone within 50 feet of you see your public GitHub profile, browse your repos and add you to their device contacts. It also sends you notifications when other users are nearby and gives you a history of the people you've crossed paths with.

Proximity detects with Bluetooth LE.

Finds users even if their app is closed.

Good for meetups and conferences.

Free and open source.

Does not track by location.

Does not share your data.

Does not drain your battery.
